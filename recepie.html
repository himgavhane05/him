Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1.1/2 cups raw pasta
  • Capsicum , Bell peppers chopped in smal cube 1/4 cup
  • Zucchini chopped in small cube 1/4 cup
  • 1 tsp. oil
  • Sweet corn 3-4 tblsp
  • Cherry tomato 4-5
  • Garlic chopped 3 cloves
  • Onion chopped 1
  • Pepper powder 1 tsp
  • Red chili flakes as needed
  • salt
  • tomato pasta sauce
  • Water 1/2 cup reserved from the cooked pasta.
  • mozzarella cheese for garnishing

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta as per the instructions of the package. Reserve half cup of the cooked pasta water for later use.
  2. In a pan add the oil (you can use normal oil too) and add the chopped garlic and onions.
  3. Saute till the garlic turns golden brown.
  4. Add the chopped Zucchini and bell peppers.
  5. Add the pepper powder, red chili flakes and salt.
  6. Cook for few minutes.
  7. Add the sweet corn and mix well.
  8. Cook for 2 minutes.
  9. Add the tomato pasta sauce now.
  10. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes.
  11. Add the cooked pasta to this now.
  12. Mix well and cook for a minute.
  13. Add the cheese
  14. Serve the pasta
