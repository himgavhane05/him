HALWA

Ingredients

  • rava - 1cup
  • sugar - 1.1/2 cup
  • water - 2 full cup
  • cardamom powder - 1 tsp
  • almond pieces - 1 small bowl
  • cashew pieces - 1 small bowl
  • raisins - 1 small bowl
  • ghee - 1/2 cup

Directions

  1. heat the frying pan and add ghee.
  2. Add rava in it and stir well for 3-4 min till it become brownish.
  3. in another bowl, add water and boil at moderate temperature.
  4. in frying pan add boiled water and stir it well
  5. add sugar, cardamom, almond granule, cashew granule or raisins.
  6. mix well for 1-2 min till water is fully soaked
  7. serve
