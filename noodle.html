NOODLE

Ingredients

  • oil - 1tsp
  • carrot - 1 chopped finely
  • beans - 10 chopped finely
  • 4 capsicum - 1/4 cup chopped finely
  • water - as needed

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add veggies and saute them for 3 to 4 mins till raw smell leaves.
  2. Add in noodles cakes and boiling water. Let it cook for 2 to 3 mins.
  3. Finally add in tastemaker and mix well.
  4. serve.
